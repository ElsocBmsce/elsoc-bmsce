Who are we?

ELSOC, the Electronics Society of BMSCE, is
the student body of the department of Electronics and Communication Engineering.

WHO IS THIS FOR?

ELSOC is primarily for the students and faculties of the department of the Electronics and Communication Engineering.
Nonetheless, ELSOC wholeheartedly welcomes everyone driven by their passion for electronics.

What is our "Vision"?

ELSOC is committed to creating a dynamic and enriching environment that fosters technical excellence, innovation, and overall growth among its members through a holistic approach.

What inspired us to come up with this?

The urge to think out of the box.
The curiosity which was killed due to lack of resources.
The need of a structured format to teach students about modern technologies and their requirements.

About Us

WE ARE THE STUDENT COMMUNITY OF THE DEPARTMENT OF ELECTRONICS AND COMMUNICATION AT B.M.S COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING!

WE FOCUS ON CONDUCTING VARIOUS EVENTS AND WORKSHOPS ON VARIOUS CUTTING - EDGE TOPICS! WE AIM TO BUILD A GENERATION WHICH IS READY TO PRODUCE INNOVATIVE TECHNOLOGIES IN THE FIELDS OF VLSI, ROBOTICS, AI, ETC.
