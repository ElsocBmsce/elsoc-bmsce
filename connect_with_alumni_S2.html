Connecting with alumni- Session2 : Graduate studies in USA and SYSTEM SECURITY System

For the second episode under this series, we invited Mr. Aravind Prakash who graduated from the department of Electronics and Communication BMSCE in the year 2004. Dr. Aravind Prakash is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Binghamton University with over a decade of experience in software R&D experience from top companies like Microsoft and is the winner of the prestigious CAREER award by the US National Science Foundation. The talk was informative and interactive & was held on 14th August 2021 at 6:30 PM IST on the platform Cisco Webex. The talk had a total of 91 registrants and 65 attendees .

The talk was graced by Dr. Arathi R Shankar, HOD, ECE department, and the faculty coordinators of ELSOC, Dr. Rajath Vasudevmurthy and Dr. Suma M N. Starting off with an introduction by Meghana GK, the president of ELSOC followed by an introduction by Dr Suma. During the first half of the webinar the speaker covered topics such as how to pick between research and industry, how to approach the job market in the USA, what to look for in a master's University, what to look for in a PhD University & how to pick a research area. The second half of the webinar included the speaker giving insights about his research on operating systems, reverse engineering, memory forensics, android and program integrity. At the end, the podium was opened up to the audience for a question and answer session which found enthusiastic participation.The talk was finally addressed by our HOD Dr Arathi Shankar and our faculty Dr Meera. Meghna GK concluded the event at 7:30 PM with a vote of thanks and a photo session with the attendees
