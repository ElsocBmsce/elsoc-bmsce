Connecting with alumni- Session1

In this series, alumni will be provided with a platform to share their knowledge regarding their field of expertise and serve as an inspiration for students.

For the first session of this series, we invited Mr. Vikram Sharma Mailthody, who graduated from the department of Electronics and Communication in the year 2013 from our esteemed institution. Currently, Mr. Vikram is a PhD Candidate at Electrical and Computer Engineering Department of University of Illinois, Urbana Champaign and works with the IMPACT Research Group led by Prof. Wen-mei Hwu. His expertise being machine learning applications to system architecture, the talk focused on Career opportunities and trends in the domain of VLSI and ML and their interdependency. The talk was held on 7th, July, 2021 at 7 PM IST on the platform Cisco Webex.
